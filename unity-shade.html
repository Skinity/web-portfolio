Insight

Shade is a 2D side-runner where the players can switch between two parallel universes.

The game explores the journey of Shade, a boy searching for colour in a lifeless world. Players must time their jumps and dimension switches to navigate obstacles that only exist in one universe or the other, guiding Shade toweards the end of each level ("dark door").
